Optimise energy costs through local renewable generation, storage, dynamic grid tariffs, and more. Explore scenarios to develop intuition in an uncertain future. Specify your location and demand, `technoeconomics-app` handles the rest.

Currently, we support:

  - low temperature industrial process heat

Support is also planned for:

  - electrified households with heat pumps, EV's, solar panels, battery storage
